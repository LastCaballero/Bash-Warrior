1. Install a terminal emulator on Android

2. On your Android-Phone make a fifo-file:

            mkfifo connection

3. After this also on your Android-Phone : 
            
            cat connection | sh 2>&1 | nc -l -p 4000 > connection

4. After this you can connect with other devices to your Android-Phone with netcat:
            
            netcat ipofphone 4000
